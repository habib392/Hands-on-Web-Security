Yeh task **Shodan** ke baare mein hai. Aasan lafzon mein samjhein toh jaise Google aam websites ke pages ko search karta hai, **Shodan internet se connect hone wali har "Device" ko search karta hai.** Isi liye isko **"Search Engine for the Internet of Things (IoT)"** kaha jata hai. Yeh har waqt poore internet ko scan karta rehta hai aur check karta hai ke kahan kaun sa router, traffic camera, server, ya industrial system chal raha hai.

Chalein isko points mein break karte hain ke yeh kaise kaam karta hai aur is task mein kya bataya gaya hai:

### 1. Banner Grabbing (Information Nikalna)
Jab Shodan kisi device ko scan karta hai, toh woh us device se milne wale response (headers) ko save kar leta hai.
 * **Example:** Agar aap Shodan par apache 2.4.1 search karenge, toh internet par jitne bhi servers is specific version ko chala rahe hain, Shodan unki list nikal ke de dega.

 * **Security Use:** Penetration testing ke doran agar aapko pata chal jaye ke target company Apache 2.4.1 use kar rahi hai, aur aapko maloom ho ke is version mein koi specific **CVE (vulnerability)** hai, toh aapka kaam bohot aasan ho jata hai.

### 2. Shodan Query Filters (Sahi Target Dhoondna)
Shodan par aam search karne se lakhon results aayenge. Is liye specific results ke liye **Filters** use hote hain:
| Filter | Kaam Kya Hai? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **country:** | Sirf kisi aik mulk ke results dekhne ke liye (e.g., IE = Ireland, PK = Pakistan). | country:IE |
| **port:** | Kisi specific port par chalne wali devices dhoondna (e.g., SSH ke liye port 22). | port:22 |
| **org:** | Kisi specific company ya organization ke IP addresses dhoondna. | org:"Amazon Web Services" |
| **hostname:** | Kisi specific domain ya sub-domain ko target karna. | hostname:fakebank.thm |
Aap inko mila kar bhi likh sakte hain, jaise: apache 2.4.1 country:PK port:80 (Iska matlab hoga Pakistan mein chalne wale woh servers jo port 80 par Apache 2.4.1 use kar rahe hain).

# Some Important Details 

### 1. Web Penetration Testing mein Shodan kab kaam aata hai?
Cyber security mein jab aap kisi company ya website par testing shuru karte hain, toh us ke **5 core stages** hote hain. Shodan aap ko sab se pehli aur sab se important stage par kaam aata hai:
 
**Reconnaissance (Information Gathering)**.
Web pentesting mein iski do tarah ki types hoti hain:

 * **Passive Reconnaissance (Chup kar information nikalna):** Shodan ka sab se bada faida yeh hai ke aap target website ya server par aik bhi request bheje bina (bina touch kiye) us ka poora data nikal sakte hain. Aap target ka IP ya domain Shodan par dalte hain aur aap ko pata chal jata hai ke us par kaun si ports khuli hain, kaun sa web server chal raha hai, aur us mein kaun si purani vulnerabilities (CVEs) hain. Target company ko kaano kaan khabar nahi hoti ke koi unhein scan kar raha hai.

 * **Asset Discovery (Sub-domains aur Servers dhoondna):** Bari companies ke sirf aik website nahi hoti, un ke hazaron sub-domains aur background servers hote hain. Shodan ke filters (jaise hostname:target.com) use kar ke aap un ke woh hidden servers dhoond sakte hain jo unho ne internet par bhoolay se khulay chor diye hote hain aur jin par security kam hoti hai.

### 1. Shodan aur Nmap ka Fark (Agar Shodan sab kuch karta hai toh Nmap kiun?)
Lagta aisa hi hai ke jab Shodan sab bata raha hai toh Nmap ki kya zaroorat? Lekin in dono mein aik bohot bada aur zameen-asman ka fark hai: **Shodan Past (Purana) Data hai, Nmap Present (Live) Data hai.**
| Feature | Shodan | Nmap |
|---|---|---|
| **Data Kab Ka Hota Hai?** | Shodan ne target ko **kuch din ya hafte pehle** scan kiya tha. Yeh purani history (cached data) dikhata hai. | Nmap usi waqt **live aur real-time** scan karta hai. Jo is waqt chal raha hai, woh dikhayega. |
| **Internal Network** | Shodan sirf un devices ko dekh sakta hai jo **public internet** par hain. | Nmap se aap kisi company ke **andaruni network (local network)**, Wi-Fi, ya private servers ko scan kar sakte hain jahan Shodan nahi pohanch sakta. |
| **Customization** | Aap script badal nahi sakte, jo Shodan ne save kiya bas wahi milega. | Aap Nmap ke NSE (Nmap Scripting Engine) use kar ke apni marzi ka deep scan ya brute-force kar sakte hain. |

> 💡 **Asal Tarika:** As a Pentester, aap pehle **Shodan** use karte hain (passive recon) taake target ka aik aam idea ho jaye bina unhein touch kiye. Phir jab aap attack karne ke liye bilkul taiyar hote hain, toh aap **Nmap** chalate hain taake confirm ho sake ke jo port Shodan par khuli dikh rahi thi, kya woh *is waqt* bhi live khuli hui hai ya company ne use band kar diya hai.
